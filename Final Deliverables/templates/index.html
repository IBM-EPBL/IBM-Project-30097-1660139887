Read About Us

WE HIRE is the most innovative online job portal in India. Founded in 2022, over the past decade, WE HIRE has become a prominent name in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the portal is evident from the fact that it has crossed the 34 lakhcandidate landmark and has more than 1 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on the site.

WE HIRE connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, WE HIRE focuses on the entire career growth of candidates. To this end, WE HIRE has launched WE HIRE Learning- India’s largest career skills site for working professionals with over 500+ courses & certifications.

As the industry shifts towards mobile, WE HIRE is leading the transition and is the fastest growing job portal on mobile devices, witnessing a 100% YOY growth in mobile traffic and also offers on-the-go jobs through the WE HIRE Job Search App.
